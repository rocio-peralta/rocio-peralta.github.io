About Me

Hi there!

My name is Rocio and I'm originally from Argentina, but I've been living in beautiful New Zealand for almost 8 years now. Recently, I started teaching myself how to code and I quickly fell in love with it. I was so passionate about learning more that I decided to join the DEV Academy to further excel my potential.


This blog is all about my journey in the world of programming. I'm excited to share my experiences, the challenges I face, and the triumphs I achieve along the way. Aside from coding, I enjoy hiking, spending time with animals, and working with children.


My ultimate goal is to gain strong coding skills that will allow me to contribute to society in a meaningful way. Whether it's building useful applications, solving complex problems, or just making people's lives a little bit easier, I'm excited to see where my newfound passion for coding takes me. Thank you for joining me on this journey!
